Meet the Newhouse professor who specializes in digital journalism

I joined Syracuse University's Newhouse School in 2007 as the general manager of the Collaborative Media Room and have since been promoted to faculty as a Professor of Practice for Magazine, News & Digital Journalism.

Recently, I was tapped as the school's Executive Editor and charged with building media industry partnerships and opportunities for our students.
